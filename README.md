# Box-Knock-Down-Game

Box Knockdown — Carnival Arcade Game (20 Levels)

HOW TO PLAY (PC / laptop with mouse or trackpad):
- Click and HOLD on the play area, drag downward/backward like a slingshot,
  then let go to throw the ball. A short drag = soft throw, a long drag =
  powerful throw. It is a click-and-drag, not a single click.

HOW TO PLAY (phone/tablet touch):
- Same idea: touch and drag your finger, then lift to throw.

Just double-click index.html to open it in your browser (Chrome/Edge/
Firefox/Safari). Needs internet the first time to load the graphics
library and fonts; plays offline after that.
